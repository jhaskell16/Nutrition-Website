The Key To A Healthy Living Is A Well Balanced Diet

The Importance of Healthy Eating

What you eat effects how you feel and operate everyday. Good nutrition is the foundation of a healthy lifestyle and, balanced with physical exercise, can help you maintain your desired weight and reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease.

About 1/3 (33.8%) of American adults are obese and about 12.5 million (17%) of children 2-19 yrs. are also obese. Even those with a healthy weight are at risk of serious illness due to unhealthy eating. Teaching kids healthy nutrition at a young age is vital as food habits more than often carry over into adulthood.
